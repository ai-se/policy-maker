Policy Office, Division of Grants and Agreements

National Science Foundation
Regional Grants Conference
Fall 2003

The first NSF Regional Grants Conference of FY 2004, hosted by the University of Minnesota, will take place from November 10 - 11, 2003 at the Radisson Hotel Metrodome in Minneapolis, Minnesota. An optional pre-conference training workshop on the FastLane system for researchers and administrators will be held on Sunday, November 9.

Over 20 NSF staff will be on hand to present workshops and sessions on the following topics:

  • NSF's Funding and Directions;
  • Proposal Preparation;
  • Merit Review Process;
  • Cross-Disciplinary and Special Interest Programs;
  • Break-out Sessions (by Program Directorate as well as Grant and Awards); and
  • Grant Award and Administration

For additional information regarding program content, contact the Policy Office, Division of Grants and Agreements at (703) 292-8243, or via e-mail at policy@nsf.gov. For logistical information (including registration, lodging, etc.) visit the University of Minnesota website at: http://www.research.umn.edu/conferences/nsf/
